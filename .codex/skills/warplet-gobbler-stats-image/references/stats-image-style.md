# WarpletGobbler Stats Image Style

Use this art direction for the current WarpletGobbler daily stats graphic.

## Composition

- Square social image, 1200x1200 or equivalent.
- One centered translucent dark-purple glass panel with a thin violet border.
- Title small at top center.
- Hero metric `~Nx floor` centered and feed-stopping, but not oversized enough to dominate the entire image.
- Top bid block under hero.
- Three supporting stat rows:
  1. `Warplets Floor`
  2. `{N} Warplets held (+N today)`
  3. `$WARPGOBB mkt cap`
- Footer: `Farcaster is fun.`

## Background

- WarpletGobbler app-inspired, but push toward Hollow Knight abyss rather than rounded cavern.
- Black ragged top and bottom abyss edges.
- Muted deep-purple atmosphere.
- Long organic silhouette tendrils, wispy roots, irregular hanging shapes.
- Layered black/deep-purple outline shapes with subtle violet rim separation.
- Two pale glowing eyes near the top, subtle and eerie.
- A few faint blurred Warplet cards may appear in the background, but keep them subdued.

## Avoid

- No corner logos.
- No `.xyz`.
- No queue line.
- No current auction length line.
- No mouth, teeth, jaw, monster face, or weird footer shape.
- No bright crystal cavern, neon flood, high-glow background, or generic infographic look.
- No ETH logo for `Warplets Floor`.
- No single/few Warplet icon for holdings; use a crowded pile when possible.

## Badges

- `Warplets Floor`: mini Warplet floor/card/tag icon, not ETH logo.
- `Warplets held`: crowded pile of many tiny Warplets.
- `$WARPGOBB mkt cap`: cyan WarpletGobbler token-logo style badge.

Keep badges small and subtle; the text and hero metric are more important.
